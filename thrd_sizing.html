Thrd Sizing

Clothing at THRD originates from factories catering to European and American markets, resulting in size variations across different brands.

To address this, we've introduced our own THRD Sizing system.

We meticulously measure each piece and categorize it to ensure that, for instance, when you choose a 'medium', you receive a fit corresponding to that size.

While the label might indicate the original brand's sizing, we recommend relying on the THRD Size for a true fit
