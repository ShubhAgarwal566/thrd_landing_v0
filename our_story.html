Our Story

From January to July 2023, we journeyed across India, visiting numerous factories that produce garments for globally acclaimed brands. After countless meetings and tireless negotiations, we established a unique, direct partnership with these manufacturers.

Why Exclusivity

Exclusivity is not a marketing gimmick—it's a necessity. The stocks we procure are limited, and we want to build a curated community of fashion-forward individuals. This community understands the value of what they are buying and can confidently flaunt the brands they always aspired to wear, without any skepticism about authenticity.

Future

This is just the beginning. With regular drops of curated designs, the best is yet to come for our exclusive members.
