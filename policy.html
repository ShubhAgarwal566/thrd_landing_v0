Return and Exchange Policy

Initiating an Exchange: Should you wish to exchange an item, please get in touch with our customer service team at mail@thrd.store within 5 days of receipt.

Hygiene and Quality Standards: To uphold our commitment to hygiene, every exchange will undergo a thorough quality assessment by our dedicated team. Based on this assessment, the exchange request may either be accepted or declined.

We may decline an exchange if:

  • The item has been used.
  • The item is damaged upon return.

Shipping for Exchanges: Once the exchange is confirmed, customers are responsible for shipping the item back to us. Please provide the tracking ID by replying to the same email thread at mail@thrd.store.

Processing Exchanges: After receiving the returned item, we will proceed with the exchange process, which can take between 7-10 business days.

Received a Defective Product? If you believe you've received a defective item, please send us images at mail@thrd.store, and our team will respond within 24 hours.

Exchange Limitations: Please note that an item can only be exchanged once for size. While we strive to accommodate all exchange requests, we cannot guarantee size availability. If the desired size is out of stock, we will grant you store credit.

Store Credit Details:

  • Store credit will be issued in cases where the desired exchange size is unavailable.
  • The credit is valid for 2 month from the date of issuance.
  • If a return is approved, the refund will be in the form of store credit.

Cancellation and Refund

Cancellation: Regrettably, once an order is placed, we are unable to cancel it.

Refund: We do not offer refunds on any orders.



At THRD, we prioritize your shopping experience. Should you have any queries or concerns regarding our policies, please reach out to our customer support for clarity.
